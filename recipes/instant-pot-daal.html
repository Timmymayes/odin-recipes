Instant Pot Daal

Indian Daal is a hearty and quick meal full of nutrition that can be made quite simply utilizing an instant pot. This recipe is whole food plant based by design and as such has no added, salt or sugar. Serve over brown rice or even just scoop and eat with a whole wheat roti!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp coconut Oil
  • 1 Large Yellow Onion
  • 4 Cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground tumeric
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • t tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp cayanne add more if you like your food more spicy
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 1/2 cups of dried brown or red lentils
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped + extra for garnish

Steps

  1. On your instant pot, press the 'Saute' button, heat the oil
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent
  3. Stir in garlic, ginger, cumuin, tumeric, curry powder, cayenne, and muster seeds an cook for another minute until fragrent
  4. Hit the Cancel button and add lentils, water, salt, chopped totmaotes and mix to combine.
  5. Cover the Instant Pot with the lid, and close the pressure valve. Press the 'Pressure Cook' button and set the cooking time to 10 minutes.
  6. After the timer sounds, wait 10 minutes before opening the pressure valve
  7. After 10 minutes, open teh valve and stand back!!!
  8. Mix the lentil mixture and add the chopped cilantro.
  9. Stir to combine, and serve over basmati rice or with naan (regular or garlic), and top it with fresh cilantro!
